Windows window
---------------------
Move with WASD
Drag the mouse on the screen to look around.
Hold Shift to move slower.
Press ESC to quit.
---------------------
Console
---------------------
1.Type ip,then port.(ex. 127.0.0.1 and 27015)
2.Place ships one by one,by typing it's size,then a coordinate.(ex. a5)
3.After all ships have been placed,type a coordinate to shoot at,when the console asks you to.
Continue this until you,or the enemy loses all the ships.
